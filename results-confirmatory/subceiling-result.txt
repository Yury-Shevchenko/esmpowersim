Registered hypothesis
  In the sub-ceiling regime, holding expected total observations constant, higher
  between-person CV in trigger rate reduces power. CONFIRMED iff BOTH criteria hold.

Result
  Criterion 1 (reliability): empirical-logit(power_all) ~ cv + N + lambda_bar + D
    + beta1.  CV coefficient = -0.157, 95% CI [-0.430, 0.117].
    Negative AND CI upper < 0 ?  NO  (CI includes 0).
  Criterion 2 (magnitude, informative band): among the 20/32 matched pairs whose
    cv=0.3 power is in [0.2,0.8], median power gap [power(.9)-power(.3)] = -0.050.
    <= -0.03 ?  YES.
  Secondary dose-response: power non-increasing across cv in 21/32 cells (66%).

VERDICT: NOT CONFIRMED.
  The penalty's MAGNITUDE criterion was met (median -5.0 pts in the informative
  band, consistent with the parent study's exploratory -4.2 pts), but the
  RELIABILITY criterion was not: the pooled logit-regression CV coefficient,
  though negative, had a 95% CI crossing zero. Because confirmation required
  both, the hypothesis is not confirmed. The direction and size of the effect
  are consistent with a real but modest sub-ceiling penalty that this focused
  96-cell, R=2000 test could not establish as statistically reliable.
